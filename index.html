School Management System is a complete school management software designed to automate a school's diverse operations from classes, exams to school event calendar. The School software has a powerful online community to bring parents, teachers and students on a common interactive platform. It is paperless office automation solution for today's modern schools. The School Management System provides the facility to carry out all day to activities of the school, making them fast, easy, efficient and accurate.
