About School

Lockwood Elementary is a public elementary school located in Bothell, WA in the Northshore School District. It enrolls 552 students in grades 1st through 12th. Lockwood Elementary is the 313th largest public school in Washington and the 17,312th largest nationally. It has 20.1 students to every teacher. Phi Delta Kappa- WA and the Center for Educational Effectiveness selected Lockwood as one of fifty five elementary schools rated in the top 5% of highest of schools improving schools in our school.

Progress

Lockwood Elementary School is a highly rated, public school located in BOTHELL, WA. It has 645 students in grades PK, K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 17 to 1. According to state test scores, 82% of students are at least proficient in math and 84% in reading.
